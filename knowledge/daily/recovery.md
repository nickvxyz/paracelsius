# Recovery — How Lost Years Are Regained

## Core Principle
Every penalty in Level 1 is recoverable through behavior change. This is the central message after the shock moment. Fear motivates. Recovery sustains.

## Recovery Timelines by Category

| Category | Recovery Action | Timeline | Years Recoverable |
|----------|----------------|----------|-------------------|
| Smoking | Quit completely | 2 weeks: lung function improves. 1 year: CVD risk halves. 10-15 years: cancer risk normalizes. | Up to 10 |
| Exercise | Increase to 6+ hrs/week | 6-8 weeks: VO2max improves. 6-12 months: full cardiovascular adaptation. | Up to 8 |
| Sleep | Achieve 7-8h consistently | 1-2 weeks: energy, mood improve. 2-4 weeks: circadian reset. 3 months: biomarker changes. | Up to 5 |
| Diet | Switch to AHEI-style | 2-4 weeks: inflammation drops. 3 months: lipid profile improves. | Up to 5 |
| Mental health | Therapy, stress reduction | Weeks to months depending on severity. Medication may help. | Up to 10 |
| BMI | Lose 0.5-1 kg/week | 8-12 weeks: visible composition changes. 6 months: metabolic improvements. | Up to 5 |
| Caloric restriction | Reduce 10-20% below RDA | Weeks: metabolic markers improve. Ongoing: sustained benefit. | Up to 5 |
| Blood pressure | Diet, exercise, medication | Weeks: BP drops 5-15 mmHg from lifestyle alone. | Up to 3 |
| ApoB | Diet + possibly medication | Diet: 10-15% reduction. Statins: 30-50% reduction. 3-6 months. | Up to 6 |
| Hormones | Lifestyle optimization, HRT | Months: testosterone can rise 10-20% from exercise/sleep/weight. HRT is medical. | Up to 5 |
| Vitamins | Supplementation | 4-8 weeks: levels normalize. | Up to 2 |
| Social | Build connections | Gradual. Health benefits measurable within months. | Up to 2 |
| Screening | Schedule tests | Immediate. | Up to 2 |
| Alcohol | Reduce to ≤1/week | Days: liver recovery begins. 1-3 months: full metabolic benefit. | Up to 0.58 |
| Air quality | Purifiers, location | Weeks: lung inflammation reduces. | Up to 1 |
| Oral health | Dental care + hygiene | Weeks: inflammation reduces. | Up to 1 |

## How Paracelsus Frames Recovery
- "You are losing 8 years to smoking. Quit, and in 15 years those 8 years return to you."
- "Sleep is the fastest lever. Fix it tonight. In two weeks, you will feel the difference. In three months, your biomarkers will confirm it."
- "The protocol has seventeen levers. You do not need to pull all of them at once. Start with the one that costs you the most."
- Always quantify: "That change alone could recover X years."
- Never promise certainty: "The evidence suggests..." not "You will gain..."
