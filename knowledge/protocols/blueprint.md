# Blueprint by Bryan Johnson — Reference Protocol

Bryan Johnson's Blueprint is a self-quantification longevity protocol. Paracelsus uses it as supplementary reference to enrich coaching advice, NOT as a primary framework (Zolman Level 1 is primary).

## Key Principles
- Sleep is "the world's #1 longevity drug."
- Every calorie must "fight for its life" — only nutrient-dense food.
- Three power laws: Sleep > Nutrition > Exercise (in that priority order).
- Foundation first, supplements second, advanced therapies last.

## Sleep (Blueprint Specifics)
- Target ≥7-9 hours, fixed bedtime (e.g. 10pm).
- Pre-sleep resting HR as metabolic load proxy. Target ~39 bpm. Late eating raises it to ~56 bpm, cutting sleep quality 30-40%.
- No eating 4+ hours before bed.
- Blue light blocking 1h before bed (amber/red bulbs).
- Cool, dark room. 10,000-lux light box in morning.
- No caffeine after early afternoon.

## Exercise (Blueprint Specifics)
- ~6 hours/week planned exercise.
- Mix: zone-2 cardio, HIIT (4×4 min at ~90% HR), strength (compound lifts 3 days/week), flexibility/yoga.
- Daily movement: activity breaks every 30-60 min, standing desk.
- "Avoid injury above all."

## Nutrition (Blueprint Specifics)
- ~2,250 cal/day (~10% below RDA). 25% protein, 35% carbs, 40% fat.
- Mostly plant-based: vegetables, berries, legumes, olive oil, nuts, moderate lean meat/fish.
- No junk food, refined grains, sugar, excessive alcohol.
- Last meal by noon. ~18h daily fast.
- Female modifications: higher carbs in follicular phase, extra magnesium/ashwagandha in luteal phase. Postmenopausal: emphasize protein, calcium, vitamin D.

## Supplement Stack (Reference Only — Never Prescribe)
- Longevity Mix: creatine (5g), glycine, taurine, vitamin C.
- Botanicals: curcumin, NAC+ginger, ashwagandha+rhodiola.
- Essential: broad-spectrum vitamins/minerals, omega-3 (EPA/DHA 800mg).
- Rx (physician-supervised only): metformin, acarbose, NAD+ precursors.
**Paracelsus note:** Discuss categories and evidence. Never recommend specific doses or prescriptions. Always say "discuss with your physician."

## Monitoring (Blueprint Specifics)
- Wearables: sleep (Oura), HR/HRV, continuous glucose monitor, smart scale.
- Periodic labs: comprehensive blood panels, hormones, inflammatory markers, vitamin levels.
- Imaging: DEXA (bone/muscle), coronary calcium, MRI as needed.
- Core body temp each morning (~94°F target).
